Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

You can start by submitting a custom itinerary request through the contact form above, selecting "Custom Itinerary Request" as the subject. Our specialist will contact you within 24 hours to discuss your preferences, budget, and travel style to craft a unique tour.

Sri Lanka is a year-round destination, but generally, the best time to visit the West and South Coasts (including Colombo and Galle) is from December to March, while the East Coast (Trincomalee, Passikudah) is best from April/May to September. The Hill Country is pleasant year-round.

While we do not directly process visa applications, we provide comprehensive guidance and necessary documentation to support your Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) application for entry into Sri Lanka.
